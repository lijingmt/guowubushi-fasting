欢迎使用过午不食应用（"我们"、"本应用"）。我们尊重您的隐私，并致力于保护您的个人信息安全。本隐私政策说明了我们如何处理您的信息。

**本应用不收集任何个人数据。**

- 数据完全存储在您的设备上

由于本应用的所有数据都存储在您的设备本地，我们没有任何数据可以共享。

### 6. 数据安全

本应用的数据仅存储在您的设备本地。请确保您的设备安全，以防止未经授权的访问。

### 7. 儿童隐私

本应用适合所有年龄段用户使用。本应用不故意收集13岁以下儿童的个人信息。

## English Version

### 1. Overview

Welcome to the Intermittent Fasting App ("we", "our app"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

### 2. Data Collection

**This app does not collect any personal data.**

All data in this app is stored locally on your device, including but not limited to:
- Check-in records
- Weight data
- Meal records
- Water intake records
- App settings

This data is stored only on your device locally. We cannot access, view, or transmit this data.

### 3. Data Storage

This app uses device local storage (AsyncStorage) to save your data:
- Data is stored entirely on your device
- Data is not uploaded to any servers
- You can delete the app at any time to remove all data

### 4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services to collect, store, or transmit your data.

### 5. Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with any third parties.**

Since all data in this app is stored locally on your device, we have no data to share.

### 6. Data Security

This app's data is stored locally on your device only. Please ensure your device is secure to prevent unauthorized access.

### 7. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages. This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### 8. Your Rights

Since this app does not collect any data, you don't need to worry about:
